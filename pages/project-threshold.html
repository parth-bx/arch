Threshold

Academic Project2024Cultural Centre
01 — Concept

Between Inside & Outside

Threshold is a cultural centre designed around the act of transition. In many civic buildings, the entrance is a single moment — a door — but this project proposes an architecture that extends the threshold into a spatial sequence of gathering, pause, and arrival.

Inspired by the Japanese concept of ma (間) — the meaning found in the interval between things — the building mediates between the frenetic street and the quiet of the gallery within through a series of layered, semi-enclosed courts.

02 — Design Process

Mapping Thresholds in Existing Buildings

The process began with precedent studies of threshold spaces in vernacular architecture — covered bazaars, mandapas, and colonnaded arcades. These were diagrammed and codified into a set of spatial rules that informed the generation of the building section.

03 — Final Outcome

A Building That Unfolds

The final design is a two-storey cultural centre with a perforated precast concrete screen. The interior unfolds through a series of courts of increasing enclosure, culminating in a double-height gallery lit from above.
