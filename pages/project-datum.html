Datum

Creative Lab2022Installation Design
01 — Concept

A Dialogue of Form & Sound

Each installation at Beats & Bricks Studio is conceived as a dialogue between form, sound, and emotion — transforming ideas into engaging spatial narratives that connect with people on a deeper, more interactive level.

Datum explores the perception of horizontal planes in space, using suspended elements and responsive lighting to challenge the viewer's sense of balance and orientation.

02 — Design Process

Prototyping Interaction

Testing light sensors and acoustic feedback loops. Prototyping at 1:1 scale was crucial to understand how the physical structure responds to human presence. Iterating through digital simulations and physical mock-ups.

03 — Final Outcome

Engaging the Senses

  • Structural design for the suspended plane components
