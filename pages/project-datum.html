Datum

Internship Project2022Urban Masterplan
01 — Concept

A Line That Organises

Datum began as an urban design commission for a 12-hectare brownfield redevelopment in Gurugram. The masterplan proposes a linear datum — a continuous public spine — that organises the development and offers a pedestrian armature connecting all precincts.

The datum is not a road but a sequence of public rooms: a market, a square, a garden, a cultural forecourt — each with its own character, stitched together by a tree-lined promenade.

02 — Design Process

GIS Analysis to Urban Strategy

The process began with GIS mapping of movement patterns, urban density, and land use in the surrounding context. These were distilled into a set of design principles that drove the masterplan geometry.
