Membrane

Research Project2023Adaptive Façade
01 — Concept

A Skin That Breathes

Membrane is a research-led design project exploring the potential of parametrically driven façade systems that respond dynamically to environmental conditions — sun angle, wind direction, and internal occupancy.

The project draws on biomimetic principles: the way a leaf regulates transpiration, or a pinecone opens and closes with humidity, to propose a building envelope that is genuinely adaptive rather than merely decorative.

02 — Design Process

Grasshopper + Physical Prototype

The parametric model was built in Grasshopper with Ladybug tools for environmental simulation. Physical 1:5 scale prototypes were fabricated from 0.8mm aluminium sheet with laser-cut aperture patterns, tested for airflow and shading effectiveness.

03 — Final Outcome

Three Aperture States
