Membrane

Creative Project2023Set Design
01 — Concept

Atmospheric Spaces & Visual Storytelling

Beats & Bricks Studio ventures into set design, creating visually compelling environments that bring stories to life. Blending architectural precision with artistic expression, each set is designed to enhance mood, narrative, and performance — delivering impactful spatial experiences for events, shoots, and productions.

Membrane focuses on the skin as a dynamic storyteller, using layered fabrics and light to create a shifting environment for a performance piece.

02 — Design Process

Mood, Movement & Light

Studying the interaction between camera movement and set geometry. Using fabric prototypes to test transparency and light bounce. Every element is tested to ensure it contributes to the overall emotional arc of the production.

03 — Final Outcome

A Story Brought to Life

04 — My Role

Set Designer · Beats & Bricks Studio

Work Performed

  • Developed the visual language and mood-boards for the set design
  • Created 3D models and technical drawings for the production crew
  • Collaborated with the lighting director for atmospheric effects
  • On-site supervision during assembly and shoot days
