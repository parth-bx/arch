About the Studio

Beats &
Bricks

Beats & Bricks Design Studio is an emerging multidisciplinary design practice based in New Delhi. We work at the intersection of architecture, interior design, landscape, and urban planning — driven by the belief that every space has a rhythm, and we design it.

Our process is rooted in rigorous research, storytelling through drawing, and a deep respect for the cultural and climatic context of each project. We believe design is an act of care.
