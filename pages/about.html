About the Studio

ABOUT

Beats & Bricks Studio, founded by Ar. Khushi Patel, as an LLP Firm, is a multidisciplinary design practice that explores the intersection of space, process, and creativity. The studio specialises in architectural design and spatial experimentation while simultaneously embracing a parallel practice in music creation and visual storytelling.

The studio's philosophy is simple: every creative act — whether building a space or composing a track — follows a process of rhythm, iteration, and refinement. Over the past ten years, Beats & Bricks has grown from architectural projects to include concept installations, prototypes, and immersive experiences — all reflecting clarity, simplicity, and a strong connection to context.

— Design is experienced through making: physical models, prototypes, and spatial experimentation
— Music and architecture coexist as parallel practices: each informs the other conceptually, without compromising the integrity of either
— Projects extend beyond buildings: installations, exhibitions, visual storytelling, and music productions

Beats & Bricks Studio is not just an architecture firm — it is a creative lab where disciplines converge. The practice takes on projects that demand a simultaneous understanding of the built environment and a sensitivity to atmosphere, narrative, and experience.

Every commission, whether large or intimate, is approached with the same rigour: deep contextual research, an iterative design process, and a commitment to the honesty of materials and form. The studio believes that good design does not shout — it resonates.
