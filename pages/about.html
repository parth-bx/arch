About

Aanya
Sharma

I am a fifth-year architecture student at the School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi. My work sits at the intersection of spatial design, urban infrastructure, and material culture. I am drawn to projects that ask difficult questions about how we inhabit cities — not just aesthetically, but socially and ecologically.

I believe architecture is an act of care. My design process is deeply rooted in drawing and making — I trust the pencil and the physical model as much as the screen.
