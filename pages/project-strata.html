Strata

Internship Project2023Mixed-Use Development
01 — Concept

Vertical Urbanism — Programme as Geology

Strata was developed during an internship at Morphogenesis, New Delhi. The brief called for a vertical mixed-use tower accommodating retail, co-working, serviced apartments, and sky gardens within a 30-storey frame.

The design approach treated programme as geological strata — visible bands of activity stacked vertically, each with its own material language, setback profile, and access strategy.

02 — Design Process

Working in a Live Professional Studio

This was a first exposure to professional practice — contributing to the schematic design phase, specifically working on the podium-level retail planning and sky-garden interface floors. Learning to balance design ambition with structural grid constraints and client brief priorities.

03 — Final Outcome

A Tower That Changes as You Rise

04 — My Role

Intern — Morphogenesis, New Delhi · 6 Months

What I did

  • Worked on schematic design for podium retail layout (floors B1–3)
  • Developed sky-garden interface floors in AutoCAD and Rhino
  • Produced 1:50 detail drawings for façade junction conditions
  • Attended client presentation meetings and prepared model photographs
  • Assisted in preparing presentation boards for regulatory submission
