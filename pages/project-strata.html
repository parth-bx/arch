Strata

Studio Project2023Installation Design
01 — Concept

Immersive Spatial Narratives

Beats & Bricks Studio extends beyond conventional architecture into immersive installation design, crafting spaces that blend structure with sensory experience. Each installation is conceived as a dialogue between form, sound, and emotion — transforming ideas into engaging spatial narratives that connect with people on a deeper, more interactive level.

Through Strata, we explored the intersection of light and material density, creating a rhythmic pathway that guides users through a series of atmospheric transitions.

02 — Design Process

Materiality & Sound

The design process involved testing sonic qualities of materials and how they resonate within the structure. Scale models were used to study the acoustics and light filtration, ensuring a multi-sensory experience.

03 — Final Outcome

A Resonating Structure

04 — My Role

Lead Designer · Beats & Bricks Studio

Work Performed

  • Designed the modular structural system for rapid assembly
  • Supervised the fabrication and material testing phase
  • Coordinated with sound designers for the immersive audio layer
