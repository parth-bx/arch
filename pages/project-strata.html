Strata

Internship Project 2023 Mixed-Use Development
01 — Concept

Vertical Urbanism — Programme as Geology

Strata was developed during my internship at Morphogenesis, New Delhi. The brief called for a vertical mixed-use tower accommodating retail, co-working, serviced apartments, and sky gardens within a 30-storey frame.

The design approach treated programme as geological strata — visible bands of activity stacked vertically, each with its own material language, setback profile, and access strategy. The building reads differently at street, mid, and skyline levels.

02 — Design Process

Working in a Live Professional Studio

This was my first exposure to professional practice. I contributed to the schematic design phase — specifically working on the podium-level retail planning and the sky-garden interface floors. I learned to balance design ambition with structural grid constraints and client brief priorities.

03 — Final Outcome

A Tower That Changes as You Rise

Each stratum has its own material register: rough textured concrete at podium level, flush glass panels in the co-working zone, and a lighter perforated metal screen wrapping the serviced apartment band at the crown.

04 — My Role

Intern — Morphogenesis, New Delhi · 6 Months

  • Developed sky-garden interface floors in AutoCAD and Rhino
  • Produced 1:50 detail drawings for façade junction conditions
  • Attended client presentation meetings and prepared model photographs
  • Assisted in preparing presentation boards for regulatory submission
